# Policy Engine

> **Note:** Policy intelligence is a paid component. Basic policy filters are open.

## Purpose

Control which documents are included in context based on configurable rules.

## Open Features

- Basic inclusion/exclusion filters
- Source-based filtering
- Metadata-based filtering

## Paid Features (Policy Intelligence)

- Dynamic policy evaluation
- Policy conflict detection
- Compliance reporting
- Audit trails

## Policy Application

Policies are applied during context selection and must be:

- Deterministic
- Explainable
- Auditable
